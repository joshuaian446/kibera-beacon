Building Bridges, Strengthening Bonds

Last week, COPA Centre was filled with an extraordinary sense of joy as we hosted a delegation from Crossing Thresholds. The week-long visit was a beautiful reminder of the power of global connection and the tangible impact of shared vision.

A Week of Transformation

• New Play Area: A vibrant, safe space where our youngest learners can develop physical skills and friendships.
• School Refresh: Classrooms received a fresh coat of paint, brightening the learning environment with colors of hope.
• Community Sports Day: A massive gala where students and volunteers bonded through football and sack races.
